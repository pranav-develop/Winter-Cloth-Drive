There are 1.77 million homeless people in India. In the winter season when at night you sleep within your blankets with hot water, some people around you don't even have proper clothing to wear on. We aim to let no person in Jhansi sleep without a proper clothing / blanket on. Come forward and help us by any means.

Donate Us Money

Each rupee of the donation goes either in bringing blankets or in mending the old clothes .The amount donated gets reflected on our website and each donation can be seen within 24 hours of donating. Donations are accepted via Paytm / Gpay / Online banking / Cash.

Donate Us Clothing

You can help us by donating sweaters, jackets, jeans, blankets, shoes, socks, caps, scarfs or any other warm cloth. The clothing should be in quite proper condition. However, we will mend if any chain is broken or a small hole is present. We will also be creating blankets out of the proper warm clothes received.
